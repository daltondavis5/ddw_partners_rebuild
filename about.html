About Us

Who We Are

At DDW Partners we pride ourselves on Customer Service and Satisfaction. We want your buying experience to be an exciting and happy experience along with being as quick and smooth as possible. Each car is hand-picked and thoroughly inspected. We deal with a broad spectrum of vehicles from hybrids, economy, luxury, exotics and even classic cars. We also have extensive knowledge in the motor sports industry and can help buy, sell or trade your toys: Race cars, Boats, Sand Rails, UTV's, Motorcycles, Trailers, RV's, etc.

We maintain very high standards to maintain our reputation and we go above and beyond for our customers. We have perfect eBay feedback and we pride ourselves on that feedback because over 90% of the cars we sell that ship to other states ship without the customers ever seeing them or driving them prior to purchasing. That speaks volumes to our full disclosure about each and every vehicle we sell and our customer satisfaction.

DDW Motorsports is a division that specializes in modifying and customization of your cars and toys. We deal only with the best manufactures in the world. The owner of DDW Partners has built some of the fastest street cars in the world and has made over 1500hp to the tires on his personal car. Not only do these cars go fast reliably, they look like show cars. The attention to detail and meticulous build quality are second to none.

Please don't hesitate to call us or email with any questions. We look forward to hearing from you.
